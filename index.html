CatWISE landing page

CatWISE is an all-sky catalog based on the combination of catalog sources from WISE 2010 and NEOWISE 2013-16. Similar to allWISE, CatWISE includes for every source its brightness, position, and motion. However, CatWISE reveals fainter sources and provides far more accurate measurements of their motions because it is based on 4x the raw data quantity spanned over a 10x greater timespan.
